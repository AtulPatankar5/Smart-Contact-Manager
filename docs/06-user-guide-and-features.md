# 06. User Guide & Features

This end-user guide describes all core features available in **Smart Contact Manager**.

---

## 👤 1. Account Registration & Login

### Registering an Account
1. Click **Signup** on the navigation bar.
2. Fill in your **Name**, **Email**, **Password**, and a brief **About** description.
3. Check the terms and conditions box.
4. Click **Submit**. If inputs are valid, your account is created with default role `ROLE_USER`.

### Logging In
1. Click **Login** on the top menu.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Upon authentication, you are redirected to your personal dashboard (`/user/index`).

---

## 📞 2. Contact Management

### Adding a New Contact
1. From the dashboard sidebar, click **Add Contact**.
2. Complete contact details:
   - First Name / Nick Name
   - Work / Position
   - Email
   - Phone Number
   - Profile Image Upload (optional)
   - Detailed Contact Description
3. Click **Save Contact**.

### Viewing Contacts with Pagination
1. Click **View Contacts** in the sidebar.
2. Contacts are displayed in a clean table (5 contacts per page).
3. Use the page navigation buttons at the bottom to browse through contact pages.

### Viewing Contact Details
1. In the contact list table, click on any contact's email or detail icon.
2. A detailed modal/card displays complete contact information including profile picture, full name, email, phone, occupation, and notes.

### Updating a Contact
1. In the contact table or detail view, click **Edit**.
2. Update the desired fields or upload a new profile picture.
3. Click **Update** to save changes.

### Deleting a Contact
1. Click the **Delete** button next to a contact.
2. Confirm the action when prompted. The contact entry and its associated media are removed from your account.

---

## 🔓 3. Password Reset via OTP (Forgot Password)

If you forget your password:
1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address and click **Send OTP**.
3. A 4-digit One-Time Password (OTP) will be sent to your email inbox.
4. Enter the received OTP on the verification screen.
5. Enter your new password and submit to update your credentials securely.
